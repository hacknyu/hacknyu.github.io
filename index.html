HackNYU organizes events at NYU around building creative tech.

About

HackNYU puts together events to help grow a positive maker and hacker community at NYU and other nearby schools. We are an official NYU student run organization, and work with sponsors and the university to bring you our events free of charge.

Our largest event (which shares our name), HackNYU, brings together over 500 students for 48 hours every year to create projects that address pertinent global issues.
